Cyber Security

Pre-requisites

  • Must know how to use a computer.
  • Passionate about learning creative and challenging concepts.
  • Basic knowledge of Web and Internet.

Tell me about it

Learning about Cyber Security will help you protect crucial information and deny unauthorized control over your system. This shall save you from being answerable and from being cheated over by a malicious user through various malpractices. Ethical Hacking will let you know the ways in which a hacker can access your system. This will allow you to secure yourself and shield yourself from all the threats you are prone to.

Unwrapping the workshop

This workshop is strategically designed to impart the right knowledge to an aspiring ‘Hacking and Cyber Security’ enthusiast. One shall explore the various means that an intruder can have the access to computer resources. Vulnerability and attacks that the system is prone to shall be investigated through a brief theoretical study of the same. A few possible attacks will be demonstrated as well. Counter measures to ensure security of the system is the main aim tied to this workshop.

Course Content

  • Importance of Information Security
  • Introduction to evolving technologies
  • Information Gathering
  • E-mail Attacks and Security
  • Malware Illustration
  • Web Application Attacks
  • Google Dorks
  • Secure yourself on Internet
  • Metasploits
  • Reverse Engineering
  • Buffer overflow

Personality Development

Pre-requisites

  • An attentive mind.
  • Passion to learn and implement these tricks in ones’ own life.
  • Respect for oneself.

Tell me about it

First impression is the last impression. It won’t take a long time for your associate to judge you on your first meeting. Your personality proves to be an easy reflection of your inner self. Your behavior, actions, gestures and speech combine and make your personality. Tricks and tips from mentors, who have looked at life closely and have various incidents to back their personality strength, is what one needs for their own betterment.

Unwrapping the workshop

Through this workshop, you will be able to understand the manner in which people perceive or judge you. A briefing with the common mistakes that one makes during their first interaction with another individual shall be mentioned. Tips and tricks along with their importance will be discussed and thought upon.

Web Designing

Pre-requisites

  • Basics of HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

Tell me about it

The Web now holds the identity of each and every individual. Leave your mark on it too. HTML, CSS and JavaScript combined have the capability to create a magic over the browser. You got to learn to be the Magician. Every one might know the trick, but its time you use them the right way.

Unwrapping the workshop

This Workshop would help aspiring designers to make use of their basic knowledge about the web- concepts by getting to know those links and loops that can be used to the fullest. A brief introduction and use of BootStrap will be discussed. Also how to set up a creative layout for your websites will be shown. The workshop serves the purpose of motivating and acquainting you with the in-and-out of web designing.

RC Planes

Pre-requisite

  • Must know the basics of working with electronics.
  • Should be able to work with wires, solder, etc.

Tell me about it

A workshop on RC (Radio – controlled) Plane is a fun learning experience for one and all. It can be worked upon as a hobby for many. And is of great use to the scientific, military and government organizations. It is a topic of interest but is challenging too.

Unwrapping the workshop

This workshop aims at given each of its participants a brief exposure to the basics of flying a RC Plane, the components used and their working. Flying experience will be given with the help of a simulator. A working plane will be shown. (We may/ or may not be able to cover the complete assembling and flying of the plane.)
